Mission Statement

Traffic congestion is a constant challenge that affects travel times, air quality, and overall efficiency. With population growth, economic development, and other continuous changes, the impact of traffic is becoming more problematic for travelers with each passing day.

The plan is to create a website that navigates real-time traffic data, enabling users to view current traffic conditions. This platform will offer a variety of useful tools, such as route optimization, commuter alerts, and instant data access. Featuring more advanced capabilities than Apple Maps, it will provide frequent updates on traffic and route changes, addressing the lag and inaccuracies often seen with Apple Maps. The map will utilize a color-coded system to organize information by category, helping users quickly assess the best course of action. Precision will be achieved through satellite connectivity.
